Features

Boards

Projects are represented as boardss in the system. Along with displaying essential information about the project. They can be set to public or pirvate, depending on the user's preferences.

Tasks

Tasks and substasks are the smallest chunks of executable assignments. Tasks can be assinged to multiple users, given a priority and easily moved across different states in the development pipeline.

Members

Users invited to work together with the common goal of deliverirng a succssful project. They can have serveral different roles as active members of the development, or simply followers of the board.

Timer

The time tracking feature enables the users to easily measrue the time spent on a specific task. One tracked and logged, the time can be seen and edited in a dedicated Time Records view for additional control.

Follow

Ahead offers the possibility to follow the development of a project and benotified of tis activities. Users can follow entire boards, or they can choose to follow just specific tasks within a board.

Recently Accessed

The Boards that you are actively working on can be easily accessed with the Recently Accessed feature.
